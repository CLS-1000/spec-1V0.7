## Usage Tips

1. **Personalize:** Use their name, reference their company/domain, show you understand their challenge
2. **Lead with benefit:** Not features — what problem does SPEC-1 solve for them?
3. **Keep it short:** Aim for 150–200 words in initial email; save details for call
4. **CTA:** Always ask for something specific (call, pilot, intro, feedback)
5. **Follow up:** If no response in 5–7 days, send one follow-up. Then move on.
6. **Track:** Use email tracking or CRM to monitor opens/clicks and prioritize warm leads
7. **Iterate:** Test different subject lines, lengths, value props — see what gets responses
