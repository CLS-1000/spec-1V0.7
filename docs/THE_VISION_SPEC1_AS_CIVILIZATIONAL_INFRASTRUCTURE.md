**Not another OSINT tool. Not another local newsletter.**

---

## The Fundamental Problem

Civilization is currently running on extremely degraded sensory apparatus.

We have:
- Massive amounts of raw data
- Extremely sophisticated actors who specialize in manufacturing synthetic reality at scale (narrative warfare, astroturfing, regulatory capture, coordinated discrediting)
- Almost no reliable, distributed, high-resolution sensors for "what is actually happening with power in this specific place"

The result is that small groups of highly coordinated people can steer outcomes in cities, states, and nations with very little resistance, because almost nobody has a clear picture in real time.

This is not a journalism problem.
This is not a technology problem.
This is a **sensory infrastructure problem**.

---

## The First-Principles Insight

If you want a healthy civilization, you need reliable ways for humans (and later, AI systems) to perceive reality at the relevant scales.

Physics gives us telescopes and microscopes because we understood we needed better eyes for the very large and very small.

We have almost nothing equivalent for the social/political/economic domain — especially at the local and regional level where most actual power is exercised and most damage is done.

Current solutions are terrible:
- National media: Too coarse, captured, slow
- Enterprise intelligence platforms: Extremely expensive, opaque, designed for states and corporations
- Social media: Actively optimized for narrative velocity over truth
- Individual researchers: Heroic but fragmented and under-powered

4. **Locally rooted** (the highest resolution and most important work happens at the scale where people actually live)

The modularity you originally asked about is not just good engineering.

It is the only known architectural pattern that allows intelligence infrastructure to scale without immediately being captured by the most powerful actors.

SPEC-1 should evolve into the reference implementation of **Planetary-Scale, Locally-Grounded Truth Infrastructure**.

Not one company owning all the sensors.

A composable, open-protocol substrate where:
- Different groups can run different "senses" (regional desks, issue verticals, specialized anomaly detectors)
