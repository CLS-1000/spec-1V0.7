# Anomaly Layer — Product Concept

**The early warning system for narrative and influence attacks in your region.**

---

## What It Is

SPEC-1 already runs multiple independent anomaly detectors:

- **cls_psyop**: 5-pattern psychological operation scorer (PSYOP_CANDIDATE / CONFIRMED)
- **cls_journalist**: Timing and signature detection on coordinated discrediting campaigns against reporters
- **cls_pdx1 anomaly module**: Geographic and entity-based anomaly detection tied to real local government data

These are currently internal.

We surface them as a distinct, high-signal product.

---

## Why This Is Powerful

Most "misinformation" or "narrative" tools are either:
- After-the-fact fact-checks (too slow)
- Vague "trust scores" (untrustworthy themselves)

This system looks for **structural signatures** of coordinated campaigns in real time, using the same deterministic + auditable logic as the rest of SPEC-1.

---

## Initial Product

- "Anomaly Alerts" thread on X when high-confidence patterns are detected
- Dedicated section in the Metro Citizens Brief when relevant
- Paid: Real-time alerts + entity-specific anomaly monitoring

---

## Long-term Vision

This becomes one of the core "senses" of the platform — the part that tells you when someone is trying to manufacture reality in your region, not just what the reality is.
