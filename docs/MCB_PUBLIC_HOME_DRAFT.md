## What is the Metro Citizens Brief?

Every morning, SPEC-1 ingests data from Oregon and Washington legislative systems (OLIS), campaign finance records (ORESTAR), and other public sources.

It runs every signal through a deterministic 4-gate filter (Credibility, Volume, Velocity, Novelty), applies a neutrality gate, and surfaces only the highest-signal developments in a clean, auditable format.

No black boxes. No spin. Just the facts, structured so you can see the connections.

**Current Coverage:** Multnomah, Washington, Clackamas (OR) + Clark County (WA)

**Mission:** Close the intelligence access gap for journalists, small business owners, civil liberties advocates, and independent researchers in the region.
