## THE FINDING

The Hwasong-11A is not a copy.

That single sentence rewrites the risk profile for every air defense system
the West has sold or promised to Ukraine — and it rewrites it in real time,
with active warfare as the test environment.

Ukrainian defense officials confirmed in April 2026 that the KN-23 and KN-24
missiles recovered from Russian strike sites are indigenous North Korean
designs. Not reverse-engineered Iskanders. Not licensed Russian production.
North Korean. Built with commercial electronics and manufacturing methods
decades behind modern standards — and yet flying. Hitting targets. Improving
in accuracy since 2023.

The story is not the missile. The story is what the missile proves about
North Korean design capability, and who is now paying attention to that proof.
